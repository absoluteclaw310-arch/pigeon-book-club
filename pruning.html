Expert Tree Pruning

Serving all of the Capital District, Urban Tree Fellers provides professional tree pruning services designed to protect your property and extend the life of your trees.

Tree pruning is not just cosmetic—it’s essential maintenance. Without it, trees can become overgrown, structurally weak, and dangerous, especially during storms common throughout Upstate New York.

Certified Professional Arboriculture Standards

Why Tree Pruning Is Critical

Prevent Property Damage

Dead or overextended branches can break without warning. In areas like Albany and Niskayuna, where storms and heavy snow are common, unmaintained trees are one of the leading causes of property damage.

Stop Disease Before It Spreads

Diseased limbs don’t heal—they spread infection throughout the tree. Strategic pruning removes these risks early.

Improve Growth and Longevity

Proper pruning redirects energy into healthy growth, strengthening the structure of the tree over time.

Increase Curb Appeal

A well-maintained tree enhances the overall appearance and value of your property, especially in neighborhoods like Delmar and Slingerlands.

Our Professional Process

We use precise, industry-standard techniques—not harmful shortcuts like topping. Our team evaluates each tree individually, removing only what’s necessary to improve health, safety, and structure.

Don’t Wait Until It Becomes a Hazard

Tree issues don’t fix themselves—they get worse over time.
