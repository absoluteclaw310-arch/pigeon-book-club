The Pigeon Show

Join us for a public learning experience. Our members will be on hand to introduce you to these amazing birds and share the secrets of their breeds.

The Racing Homer

The legendary athletes of the sky. Bred for unmatched speed and an uncanny homing instinct, these birds are the gold standard of intelligence and endurance.

The Satinette

A masterclass in avian elegance. The Satinette is prized for its refined appearance and a lustrous, shimmering glow that captures the light from every angle.

The Tumbler & Roller

The sky's most daring acrobats. These pigeons turn flight into a performance, executing breathtaking mid-air somersaults and rolls that defy gravity.

The Indian Fantail

A true architectural marvel of nature. With its iconic fan-shaped tail and proud, regal posture, the Indian Fantail is a guaranteed showstopper.

The Fancy Pigeon

Where nature meets artistry. Fancy pigeons showcase the incredible diversity of the species, featuring exotic colors and stunning feather patterns bred over generations.

Dove Release Pigeons

The heartbeat of our most emotional moments. Expertly trained for ceremonial releases, these doves symbolize peace and new beginnings.
